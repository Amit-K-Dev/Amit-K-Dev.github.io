Excel Marketing Funnel Analysis

Built a marketing funnel dashboard in Excel to track conversion metrics and revenue distribution.

Objective

To evaluate user journey stages from impressions to final revenue and identify drop-off points in the funnel.

Metrics Tracked

  • Click-Through Rate (CTR)
  • Stage-to-stage conversion rate
  • Customer acquisition cost estimation
  • Revenue split (New vs Returning)

Technical Skills Applied

  • Advanced Excel formulas
  • Pivot Tables & Charts
  • Conditional Formatting
  • Automated KPI dashboard layout

Outcome

Identified that the largest conversion drop occurred between click and add-to-cart stages, indicating optimization opportunity.
