Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing user journey data through a marketing funnel using Excel to identify drop-off points and optimize conversion performance. To evaluate conversion rates across funnel stages and measure the impact of marketing performance on revenue generation.

Business Impact

Analysis revealed the largest drop-off occurred between click and add-to-cart stages, highlighting opportunities for UX optimization and targeted remarketing strategies.
