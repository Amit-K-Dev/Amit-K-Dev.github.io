SQL Business Insights

This project focuses on analyzing structured sales data using advanced SQL techniques to extract actionable business insights.

Objective

To evaluate revenue trends, customer segmentation, and product performance by writing optimized SQL queries on transactional datasets.

Tools Used

  • MySQL / PostgreSQL
  • Joins & Subqueries
  • Aggregation Functions
  • Group By & Window Functions

Key Analysis Performed

  • Monthly revenue trend analysis
  • Top 10 performing products by revenue
  • Customer repeat purchase behavior
  • Revenue contribution by region

Business Impact

Identified that 20% of customers contributed to over 60% of total revenue, highlighting the importance of retention strategies.
